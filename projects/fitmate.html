Problem

In Singapore, busy lifestyles, limited nutritional knowledge, and easy access to unhealthy food make it challenging for people to maintain a healthy diet, impacting their well-being. A comprehensive health and nutrition tracker application is urgently needed to address these challenges and promote a healthier lifestyle in Singapore.

Solution

The health app in Singapore benefits individuals by offering a user-friendly platform to track food intake and make informed decisions using a nutritional database. It analyzes calories and macronutrients, providing valuable insights. Personalized goals and recommendations promote sustainable lifestyle changes and physical activity. The app empowers users to monitor progress, make informed choices, and improve health outcomes, fostering a culture of healthy living in Singapore.

MySQL, MongoDB, Flask, Python
