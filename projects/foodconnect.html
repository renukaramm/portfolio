Overview

Food Waste Reduction Platform that leverages cloud-based containers and Kubernetes. Our platform will facilitate the efficient tracking and redistribution of surplus food items. There will also be a feature to match supermarkets with surplus food nearing expiration with organizations in need, promoting a sustainable and community-driven approach to reducing food wastage.

Problem

The global problem of food wastage has significant environmental and social consequences. Our motivation stems from the urgency to tackle this issue with a seamless, technology-driven solution. By bridging the gap between surplus food providers and organizations in need, we aspire to contribute to a more sustainable future.

Solution

Efficient Matching Algorithm, Real-time Tracking and Notifications, User-Friendly Interface, Logistics and Distribution Management, Data Analytics for Waste Reduction, Privacy and Security.

Tools Used

Kubernetes, MySQL, Flask

My Role

Frontend, Location finding, Database Setup
