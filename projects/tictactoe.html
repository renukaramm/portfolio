Overview

Using C programming language, create a 3x3 Tic Tac Toe game for a particular nursery childcare organization to assist their kids in the development of their motor skills, social skills and initial stage of left-brain development. The features of the 3x3 Tic Tac Toe game include one-player, where the player will play with AI enabled computer and two-player modes.
